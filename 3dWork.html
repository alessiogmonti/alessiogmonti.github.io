These structures are explorations of algorithmic design made in Rhino, using its plugins Grasshopper, Weaverbird, Kangaroo2, PufferFish and more. Their 3D models are showcased in three.js, a javascript library for web-based interactive 3D display.

Algorithmic design (AD) differs from traditional computer aided design (CAD) in that CAD models are static, with explicitly defined parameters, while AD models are defined by combinations of mathematical functions with variable inputs that create fluid forms.

For this reason, these shapes are trivial to convert into data-driven models, and forthcoming work will showcase data visualization using these tools.
