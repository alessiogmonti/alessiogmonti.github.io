#005

A line-based geometry thickened and curved using Weaverbird's Catmull-Clark method to subdivide the frame built from the original line-based structure. This way, the angles from each joint in the frame were smoothened and turned into a more organic form. The individual triangular structures were generated using wave attractor points and Weaverbird's Stellate function. Upon zooming in, one can notice each individual line that is drawn- the curvature and thickness is a product of this clustering.
