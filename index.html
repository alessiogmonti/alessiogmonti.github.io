Data science is critical to modern industry, more so every day as automated workflows upgrade our productivity. Machine learning algorithms are capable of self-optimizing and identifying solutions to user-defined problems, uncovering hidden relationships and patterns by analyzing data.

Data visualization design is the study of frictionless concept communication. It focuses on how to streamline decisionmaking by creating the most intuitive shared visual framework, in order to enhance collaboration between many stakeholders with different backgrounds and domain knowledge.

At the intersection of both is the digital twin, a programmatically defined entity that replicates real-world concepts. The purpose of this website is to highlight the components of digital twins and share my research, and development in the field.

Algorithmically designed visuals are generative, data driven and fluid;



They present information defined by programmatically structured workflows.
